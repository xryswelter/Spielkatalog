Welcome to Spielkatalog!

Spielkatalog is a simple, modern tool to help track and organize your video game collection. Our goal is to eventually house the largest and most accurate video game database on the Internet. Each listing includes box art, descriptions, item numbers and more.

Why use Spielkatalog?

  • Huge database. Games, consoles, accessories and more!
  • Manage your collection, wish list and sell list.
  • Export your data to a spreadsheet at any time.
  • Active community of video game collectors.
